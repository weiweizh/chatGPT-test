About Me

Hello! My name is Weiwei and I'm a senior product designer with over 10 years of experience in the field. I'm passionate about creating elegant and intuitive designs that solve real-world problems. When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new coffee shops or hiking in the mountains.

I'm proficient in a variety of design tools, including Sketch, Figma, and Adobe Creative Suite. I'm also experienced in user research, prototyping, and design systems. I believe that great design is a collaborative effort and I enjoy working closely with cross-functional teams to create exceptional products.

My Services

End to end Product Design

From ideation to launch, I can help you create beautiful and functional products that users will love.

Usability testing

I provide usability testing services to help you identify areas of confusion in your product and provide design recommendations to improve the user experience. Usability testing involves observing users as they interact with your product and gathering feedback on their experience. By identifying pain points and areas of confusion, I can help you optimize your product and ensure that your users have a seamless experience. Contact me today to learn more about how usability testing can benefit your product design process.

UX evaluation

Heuristic evaluation involves analyzing your product against a set of established design principles or heuristics, and identifying areas for improvement. Through this evaluation, I can provide you with a detailed report highlighting issues in your product and their importance level. This report will help you prioritize which issues to address first, and ensure that your product meets high standards of usability and user experience.
